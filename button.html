Button

About Button

3 kinds of button are available. The button as a standard button for all your triggering actions. The link button with role="button" for redirecting to an internal page of your site. The link button with target="_blank" will open in a new tab for redirecting to an external site by example. You can mix and select size, color and shape.

Button models

3 button models area available, button as trigger and button trigger as link.

Button shapes

3 button shapes are available, button class="btn-*", square class="btn-square-*" and circle class="btn-circle-*".

Button sizes

5 sizes are purposed, from s to xxxl. For each size and shape just use class="btn-*-*".

Button colors

13 button colors are purposed, these 13 options have a gradient variation. Just use class="btn-*-* btn-*" or class="btn-*-* btn-grad-*".

You can check more about colors at color module.
