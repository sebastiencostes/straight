Button

About

There is 3 kinds of button. The button as a standard button for all your triggering actions. The link button with role="button" for redirecting to an internal page of your site. The link button with target="_blank" will open in a new tab for redirecting to an external site by example. You can mix and select size, color and shape.

Button models

There is 3 button models, button as trigger and button trigger as link.

Simply click and paste button trigger

                        
<button class="btn-s btn-purple" type="button">Button Trigger</button>
External Link

Simply click and paste external link

                        
<a class="btn-s btn-purple" href="#" rel="noopener" target="_blank" role="button">External Link</a>
Internal Iink

Simply click and paste internal link

                        
<a class="btn-s btn-purple" href="#" rel="noopener" role="button">Internal Iink</a>

Button shapes

There is 3 button shapes, button class="btn-*", square class="btn-square-*" and circle class="btn-circle-*".

Simply click and paste button

                    
<button class="btn-s btn-purple" type="button">Button</button>

Simply click and paste square button

                    
<button class="btn-square-s btn-purple" type="button"><ion-icon name="add-circle-outline" /></button>

Simply click and paste circle button

                    
<button class="btn-circle-s btn-purple" type="button"><ion-icon name="add-circle-outline" /></button>

Button sizes

There is 5 purposes sizes, from s to xxl. For each model and shape just use class="btn-*-*".

Internal Link External Link

<button class="btn-xxl btn-purple" type="button">Button Trigger</button>
