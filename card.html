Cards

About

Cards allows you to show some many contents. Related Informations must be short, clear and explicit. They offers the possibility to make some actions, add some medias and many more. Cards are the deal to preview your products, projects and more.

Card models

From simple to complex card, there is many opportunities to pattern your contents. Feel free to mix, customize and rearrange your content.Thanks to srcset you can play around with your media at different resolution but it's important to preserve the image dimension ratio.

When using Card Carousel you must create and keep an id on <figure id="yourCarouselID" class="carousel">.

When using Card Action you must keep the data-attr <data-card="let it blank"> on appropriate tag elements.
