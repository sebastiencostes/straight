Carousels

About

Carousels allow you to show mutli content in one component. Components are showed one by one.

Carousel models

Simple carousels are provided. There is three kinds purposed carousel. Each offers differents options about presentation. When using Carousel you must create and keep an id on each <yourtag id="yourCarouselID" class="carousel">. Thanks to srcset you can call your optimized media for specific device's breakpoint.

Simple Carousel

.carousel

Copy to clipboard

Simply click and paste simple carousel to your project
