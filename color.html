About Color

Straight Framework works with Dark and Light Theme. This theme is just here to change body's background color and body's color no more no less. This theme will automatically adapt to your Os and browser system environment. Also there is 11 colors + 1 tonality. Each of all these taints own a gradient variation and a dark and light scheme.

Flat palette

Flat palette with color's variations. Just add class="bg-*" to your item to change background color.

Gradient palette

Gradient palette with color's variations. Just add class="bg-grad-*" to your item to change background image gradient.
