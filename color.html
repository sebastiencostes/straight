About Color

Straight Framework works with Dark and Light Theme. This theme is just here to change body, nav and card body background color and body's color no more no less. This theme will automatically adapt to your Os and browser system environment. Also there is 11 colors + 1 tonality. Each of all these taints own a gradient variation and a dark and light scheme.

theme palette

Theme palette on background and color body depending on your Os/browser environment.

theme light

theme dark

Flat palette

Flat palette with color's variations. Just add class="bg-*" to your item to change background color.

Gradient palette

Gradient palette with color's variations. Just add class="bg-grad-*" to your item to change background image gradient.

text colors

text colors palette. Just add class="t-*" to your item to change text color. If you want white text just use class="t-white".

border colors

border colors palette. Just add class="b-*" or class="b-*-*" to your item to change border color. If you want white border just use class="b-white".
