Accordion

An action triggered list with extensible elements. If you want your list already open you just have to replace the accordion-close class by accordion-open class from <ul class="list-container accordion-close"> and replace caret-down-outline name by caret-up-outline name in <ion-icon name="caret-up-outline">.

You must preserve the data-accordion="" attribute on appropriate tags elements.

If you want to play around with colors you can add to <div class="accordion-container-cta accordion-*">.

If you want more collapsible elements in your list, just duplicate the <li class="list-content">.
