Accordion

An action triggered list with extensible elements. If you want your list already open you just have to remove the d-none class from <ul class="list-container d-none">. You must preserve the data-accordion="" attribute on appropriate tags elements. If you want to play around with colors you can add to <div class="accordion-container-cta accordion-*">. If you want more collapsible elements in your list, just duplicate the <li class="list-content">.
