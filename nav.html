About Nav

Use Nav when you navigate within your webapp or site. Nav represents a section of a page that has links to other internal pages or fragments of that page. It is a section intended for navigation in a document.

Nav models

2 nav models are purposed. Both are fix positionned. 1st nav works with a left slide opening to show your menu, it's a list view. This menu is vertically scrollable. 2nd is a navpad still with vertically scrollable, it's a grid view.
