modal

if you are looking for popup opening as non notification, notifications as modal are the solution. 5 sizes are available from S to XXL. They're all center displayed to window and they're all triggered. Modals are associated with triggers, in this example triggers are buttons, it shall be everything you want as soon as you keep the data attribute in in the modal itself and in the trigger itself. You can test these with these triggers just below.

<div class="notification modal-* d-none" data-notification="">

<button class="btn-* btn-* notification-open" type="button" data-notification="">
