heading

The <h1> to <h6> elements represent six levels of section headings. <h1> is the highest section level and <h6> is the lowest. Using more than one <h1> is allowed by the HTML specification, but is not considered a best practice. Using only one <h1> is beneficial for screenreader users and SEO. Heading classes are also available. You can add some classes to align your text and more...

blockquote

The <blockquote> indicates that the enclosed text is an extended quotation.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

Source Author 
            
<blockquote class="blockquote">
    <p class="blockquote-text t-justify">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p>
    <div class="blockquote-footer">
        <cite class="blockquote-cite" title="Source Title">
            <a class="blockquote-link" href="#" rel="noopener">
            Source Author
            </a>
        </cite>
    </div>
</blockquote>
