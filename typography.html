heading

The <h1> to <h6>HTML elements represent six levels of section headings. <h1> is the highest section level and <h6> is the lowest. Using more than one <h1> is allowed by the HTML specification, but is not considered a best practice. Using only one <h1> is beneficial for screenreader users. Heading classes are also available.
